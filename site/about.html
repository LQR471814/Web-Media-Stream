Web Media Stream

A project by LQR471814

"Not gonna lie I made this cause of Zoom."

I didn't have a camera or a microphone connected to my computer but I did have a camera and microphone in my laptop, sadly my laptop was laggy and I couldn't screen share anything. So I had the idea to stream the input from my laptop to my computer then use my computer to forward it to Zoom. For anyone wishing to do this you simply use OBS's (Open Broadcasting Software) virtual camera function to record the viewer only, then setup Voicemeeter Banana and use it's virtual audio CABLE to route audio from the viewer to zoom.
